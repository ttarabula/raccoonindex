Built on City of Toronto open data. Inspired by the retired Toronto Raccoon Activity Index (April 1 joke, since scrubbed; we are doing the bit now).

The index is a weighted composite of 311 service requests: wildlife cadaver and injury calls, bin-lid repairs, missed garbage pickup, illegal dumping, and overflowing litter bins — i.e., we are inferring the bandit from the ransom note. Methodology and per-ward data on the ward page.

Not affiliated with the City of Toronto. No raccoons were consulted.
